Lobectomy

What is Lobectomy?

A surgical procedure to remove a lobe of the lung due to conditions like lung cancer or infections.

Recovery Process

Recovery typically takes a few weeks, with follow-up visits and rehabilitation.

Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery

What is Bypass Surgery?

A procedure to improve blood flow by bypassing blocked arteries using grafts.

Cost & Recovery

Average cost is around $150,000. Recovery takes about 5-7 weeks.

Heart Transplant

What is a Heart Transplant?

A procedure where a diseased heart is replaced with a healthy donor heart.

Post-Surgery Care

Patients require lifelong medications and lifestyle changes.

Lung Transplant

What is a Lung Transplant?

A surgical procedure to replace a diseased lung with a healthy donor lung.

Chances of Second Surgery

Success rates vary, and patients require careful monitoring post-surgery.

How to Make an Appointment at Penn Medicine

If you have symptoms of an urgent nature, please contact your doctor, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

Schedule with my current doctor

Schedule an in-person or video visit with a doctor you have seen in the past 3 years on our patient portal, MyChart by myPennMedicine, or by calling 800-789-7366 weekdays between 8:00am and 4:30pm.

Login to MyChart by myPennMedicine

Schedule an in-person or video visit with a doctor you have seen in the past 3 years on our patient portal, My Chart by myPennMedicine, or by phone.

Login to MyChart by myPennMedicine

Or call 800-789-7366

Schedule with a new doctor

You can request an appointment with a doctor you have not seen before via our online form or by calling 800-789-7366 weekdays between 8:00am and 5:00pm.

Request an Appointment

You can request an appointment with a doctor you have not seen before by phone or online.

Call 800-789-7366

Or request an appointment using our online form.

Radiology

Request a radiology service such as an X-Ray, Mammogram, CAT scan, MRI or ultrasound, or call 215-662-3000.

Other appointment types

I would like to refer my patient

We welcome requests for patient consults, referrals and medical transfers from medical professionals.

Refer your patient

Psychiatry/Behavioral Health

For appointments with Penn Behavioral Health, call the Penn Behavioral Health Contact Center directly at 866-301-4724.

Telehealth

Telehealth visits are available for a variety of services, select new-patient appointments and follow-up care.

Current patients please reach out to your care team via MyChart by myPennMedicine.

New patients call 215-316-5145.
